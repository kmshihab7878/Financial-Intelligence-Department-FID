# Vision

AIS is an experimental autonomous trading scaffold. The target state is not an analytics assistant. The target state is a governed autonomous allocator that continuously interprets markets, constructs portfolios, executes trades, controls risk, and improves its own architecture.

## Strategic intent
- Replace fragmented human workflows with coordinated specialist agents
- Treat portfolio construction, execution quality, and risk governance as first-class intelligence functions
- Expand from simulation to constrained live deployment under explicit human mandates
- Build durable advantage from system design, not a single signal family

## Current vs target vs aspirational
### Current
A production-minded scaffold with risk-gated orchestration, conservative controls, paper-mode defaults, and audit logging.

### Target
A multi-agent investment operating layer managing approved accounts with strong attribution, strict policy enforcement, and controlled evolution.

### Aspirational
A diversified autonomous allocator operating across accounts, strategies, and regimes with institutional-grade reliability and continuous adaptation.
