# Risk Policy

## Governing principles
- No trade without explicit thesis and quantified edge.
- No exposure without portfolio context.
- No scaling without validation.
- No autonomy without hard kill-switches and auditability.
- Risk is a governing function, not a support function.

## Mandatory controls
- Hard kill-switches for daily loss, rolling drawdown, and severe execution anomalies
- Max position size caps
- Max strategy allocation caps
- Gross and net exposure caps
- Leverage ceilings
- Liquidity-aware sizing
- Concentration controls
- Auto-deleveraging triggers

## Enforcement rule
The execution stack must reject any order that lacks a valid risk approval token generated by the centralized risk engine.
