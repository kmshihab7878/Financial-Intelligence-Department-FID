# Rollout Plan

## Stage 1: Foundation
Build swarm infrastructure, shared state, decision logging, portfolio engine, risk engine, and observability.

## Stage 2: Paper and replay
Run historical replay, paper trading, slippage simulation, and crisis stress scenarios.

## Stage 3: Constrained live
Allow narrow mandates, small capital, strict exposure caps, hard kill-switches, and daily reviews.

## Stage 4: Multi-strategy expansion
Expand breadth across approved instruments and horizons only after evidence of safe autonomy.

## Stage 5: Institutional-grade autonomous management
Coordinate multiple sleeves or accounts with account-level optimization, reporting, and governed evolution.
